# STC Logo Usage

This document defines how the STC logos should be used across web pages and marketing assets.

## Approved Lockups
- **Stacked lockup**: emblem above `SOLUTIONS TREATMENT CENTER`
- **Horizontal lockup**: emblem to the left of the wordmark

## Placement Rules
- Use the **horizontal lockup** in headers and sticky navigation.
- Use the **stacked lockup** in footer brand blocks, social previews, and square formats.

## Clear Space
- Maintain a clear space equal to the height of the `S` in `SOLUTIONS` on all sides.
- No other text, icons, or decorative elements may enter this zone.

## Minimum Size
- Stacked lockup: minimum **120px** width on web
- Horizontal lockup: minimum **180px** width on web

## Color + Contrast
- The logo should remain in official full color.
- Preferred backgrounds:
  - Deep Indigo `#1F1CDE`
  - Soft Blue `#D4E3FC`
  - White `#FFFFFF`
- If placed on photos, use a dark overlay or a solid color badge/container behind the logo.

## Prohibited Usage
- Do not stretch, skew, rotate, or crop key parts of the mark.
- Do not recolor individual logo elements.
- Do not add drop shadows, glow, blur, or outlines to logo artwork.
- Do not place the logo over high-noise backgrounds without contrast support.

## Asset Inventory
Store all brand assets in `docs/brand/assets/`.

Expected files:
- `stc-logo-stacked.png`
- `stc-logo-horizontal.png`
- `stc-logo-stacked.svg` (recommended future export)
- `stc-logo-horizontal.svg` (recommended future export)
